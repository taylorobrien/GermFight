Strange Call

  • High Concept: You are helping your friend survive the medical experiment by getting the right potion from the witch.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should find the game easy until they collect the wrong potion, and then they should be frustrated due to the amount of time remaining before the player's friend dies.
  • Genre: 2d Game
  • Game play:In this prototype of Medicine Gone Wrong, the player is trying to save their friend that went through a bad medical experiment. The player must go to the flower shop, accept the job, and go collect flowers for money. After the player has enough money, the player can buy potions from the witch next door. There are five potions, and only one will help the friend survive.
  • Prototype goal:This prototype is evaluating states, multiple tilemaps, loading states, multiple scenarios, and using many objects that collide with the tilemap.
  • Features: The potion probability of guessing right.
  • Setting/Premise: The player is in a small town.
  • Story: The player has a friend in the hospital that just went through a medical experiment that is about to kill him.
  • Target Audience: This game is intended for gamers that simple fast games
  • Play time: 2-8 minutes
  • Strategies: Collect flowers quickly, and collect potions.
